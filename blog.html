Know Your Legal Rights

Indian women are protected by several legal provisions such as:

  • Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act
  • Equal Remuneration Act
  • Maternity Benefit Act
  • Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace Act

Understanding these rights is key to empowerment and equality in both professional and personal spaces.
